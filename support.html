Frequently Asked Questions

The system offers two types of reports to help track work and keep stakeholders informed: progress reports and performance reports. Progress reports are descriptive updates linked to a specific grant, allowing you to add custom subjects and sections to detail activities, milestones, or outcomes. Performance reports, on the other hand, let you select a time frame, and the system will automatically generate a report summarizing key metrics and activities for that period. Together, these tools provide a clear picture of both ongoing progress and overall performance.

"Pending" status means the grant is awaiting approval, while "Ongoing" status means the grant has been approved and is currently active.
